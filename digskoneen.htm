Requirements

The first global digital skills Championships will be officially launched, and the competition will be divided into three stages：the first stage is the online works Audition; the shortlisted competitors will enter the sub station competition for the second round competition;finally, the shortlisted competitors of each sub station competition will fight in Shanghai for the finals.

Introduction:

The following picture shows the various goblin modeling designs released by G.P. Lackey in goblin week. The author plans to reinterpret these characters to create a 3D game customized for the STADIA platform. Please select a character to further refine and process. On the premise of maintaining the character style of the game, the optimized original painting needs to clearly draw the details of the character and its equipment,the 3D model module needs rich details, PBR texture and gives the character creative actions.

Note: the contestants need to complete and submit a conceptual design drawing, a 3D modeling and mapping file that meets the requirements, and an animation file to enter the audition scoring stage.

Module A: Design description requirements

Task 1: Scheme Design:

1. According to the above role examples, competitors are required to select a role image for secondary creation. It is required to draw a set of conceptual design scheme on the premise of maintaining the same character style of the game. The optimized conceptual design chart needs to clearly draw the details of the role and its equipment. The design scheme needs to conform to the game style, and use strokes, shadows, highlights, lines and other performance body Structure, material texture, drape texture, accessories and weapon equipment and other details.

2. The character style in the design scheme cannot deviate from the original setting

3. The character modeling in the design scheme needs to hold or shoulder back weapons (equipment), and the design of weapons (equipment) should match with the overall modeling

4. The role modeling design in the design scheme should reflect the professional characteristics of the role

5. The color matching of the design scheme needs to have a clear main color and be indicated by the color matching scale map

6. To complete the drawing of the three views of the corresponding roles, it is necessary to draw auxiliary lines and mark the height scale.

7. Draw weapon (equipment) design drawings independently.

Module B: 3D Modeling and Carving

Task 1: 3D Modeling

According to the final design plan of Module A, players complete high-quality three-dimensional game role model (including clothing and props, weapons and equipment, etc.). The completed 3D character model needs to be consistent with the final design drawing, and the polygon wiring is reasonable, and the model details are in place.

Task 2: Carving

Sculpt the 3D model completed in Task 1.

Module C: UV Unwrapping and Texture Mapping

Task 1: Unwrap UVs

Unwrap the UVs and paint the map reasonably for the character completed in Model B.

Task 2: Texture Mapping

According to the selected design drawing, map the unwrap UV model in Module C Task 1, and render the static frame graph.
